Ten Questions for your Future

Posted by Clancy
  1. Is your income growing as fast as you'd lke and does it have the potential to grow in the future?
  2. In the event of retirement, the loss of a job, or unexpected catastrophic expense, do you have enough savings that would permit you to maintain your current lifestyle?
  3. Do you feel that you're in control of your daily schedule?
  4. Do you have enough time to spend with your spouse or children?
  5. Will you be content with relying on the government to take care of you and provide for your needs, or are you the kind of person who wants to stand on your own?
  6. Have you ever wanted to be your own boss?
  7. Do you believe that the the online communications industry will continue to grow for years to come?
  8. Do you know a few close friends or family members who, if you asked them, would become your online customers - especially if it saved them money and didn't change anything?
  9. Do you get personal satisfaction from helping others and from surrounding yourself with interesting and successful people?
  10. Do you strongly believe that the future can always be better than the past for those willing to work hard, take a chance and try something new?
