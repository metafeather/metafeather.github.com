Where to stay

There are loads of hotels to stay in in London, but here are a selection near the ceremony and reception venues:

  • Travel Lodge - above the Backyard Comedy Club run by our mate Dan Dearlove

What to do

If you are staying down for the weekend, and have not been to the area before here are some ideas:

  • Backyard Comedy Club - let us know and we can get you tickets
  • Italina Restaurant (review) - hosted by Eugene and with excellent handmade pizza
  • Hurwundeki - A hipster classic with excellent Korean food and BYO
  • E Pellicci - an institution for a morning after breakfast
  • Pavilion Cafe - in the park next to the lake
  • Broadway Market
  • Columbia Road Flower Market (Sunday only)
